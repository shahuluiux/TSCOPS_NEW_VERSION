Instructions To Upload Photo:

  • The Uploading picture should have a minimum 300 dpi, or a minimum of roughly 640 pixels x 480 pixels. (pre-set done in the tab with coding)
  • Pose your subjects to take a full face view (head-on)Lighting is very important; lighting should be uniform and bright,without casting shadows.
  • Natural light is best, but if you are using artificial lighting be sure it comes from several sources.
  • Fill-in flash is often very effective, even in good lighting conditions, to eliminate shadows.
  • You may be able to take very flattering pictures outdoors. However, you should not take pictures in very bright sunlight, and must avoid strong shadows.
  • Backgrounds are best when they are uniform and neutral: white or off-white is the most common.
  • Solid blue or green backgrounds are often used by professionals, as these can be easily masked out.
  • Make sure the background fills the full frame of the picture behind the person.
  • The camera should be placed at roughly the same height as the face of the subject.
  • However, if the subject wears glasses, raising the camera slightly higher will avoid
  • glare/reflection of the flash off the lenses of the glasses.
  • Frame the picture to include the shoulders, and leave some room above the head.
